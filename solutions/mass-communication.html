MESSAGE TYPES

Tailored Messages for Every Situation

Our system supports various message types and formats, each optimized for specific communication needs and emergency scenarios.

Emergency Alerts

Critical, high-priority messages that require immediate attention and response from all recipients.

Examples:
  • "LOCKDOWN: Secure all doors and remain in place until further notice."
  • "EVACUATION: Exit building immediately via nearest emergency exit."
  • "SHELTER: Move to designated safe areas immediately."

Safety Notifications

Important safety-related information that helps maintain a secure environment.

Examples:
  • "Weather Alert: Severe thunderstorm warning in effect."
  • "Maintenance Notice: Hot water will be shut off from 2-4 PM."
  • "Security Update: Visitor badges now required in all areas."

Operational Updates

Day-to-day operational information that keeps everyone informed about facility status and changes.

Examples:
  • "Schedule Change: Early dismissal today at 2:30 PM."
  • "Event Reminder: Fire drill scheduled for 10 AM tomorrow."
  • "Facility Update: North parking lot closed for repairs."

Wellness Checks

Two-way communication that allows administrators to check on staff and visitor status during incidents.

Examples:
  • "Status Check: Reply with your current location and status."
  • "Safety Confirmation: Confirm you have reached the assembly area."
  • "Wellness Survey: Rate your current safety level (1-5)."
